Golden Potato Soup

Description

This recipe give you extra-cheesy, kid-friendly potato soup.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need...

  • 3 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded American cheese
  • 1 cup chopped ham

Steps

  1. In a large stock pot add potatoes, celery, onion, chicken bouillon, water and parsley flakes. Season with salt and peppper and simmer until vegetables become tender
  2. In a separate bowl mix flour and milk. Once it is well blended, add the soup mixture and cook until soup becomes thick.
  3. stir in cheese, cooked ham or hamburger and simmer until cheese is melted.
